Featured Events

 

September 29 - 9am

Cypress Lakes Golf Club
1311 E 6th St, Muscle Shoals, AL 35661
-
Jack Karnes graduated from Springdale High School in 1996, received the Dwight Collins Memorial Golf Scholarship from Springdale Country Club in Springdale, Arkansas. Jack accepted a golf scholarship from the University of North Alabama, where he was later named to the All-Academic Team of the Gulf South Conference with a 3.81 GPA in the College of Business and was member of the Sigma Chi Fraternity. Jack had two impactful years at UNA, until his untimely death on September 20th, 1998.
In 1999, the UNA Alumni Association Homecoming Golf Tournament was renamed in Jack's memory and is now known as The Jack Karnes Memorial Golf Tournament. Proceeds from the tournament aid in underwriting the Fifth Year Scholarship Program for deserving student athletes. The purpose of the Fifth Year Scholarship is to provide continued scholarship support for student-athletes whose athletic eligibility has expired but who have not yet completed their degree requirements.

Tour of Joel R. Anderson Center for Innovation and Entrepreneurial Studies

September 30 - 1:30pm

541 W College Street

This new 6,000-square-foot facility houses The Generator, which is the Sanders College student incubator and maker’s space. The Center has more than $300,000 in equipment for students to use to create product prototypes for the business plans they are developing through the mentoring of our faculty. The Center is on the 1st floor of UNA’s new building at 541 W College Street, approximately one mile from campus. The Center is available for use by all UNA students. Parking is available.
